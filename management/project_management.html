Google Summer of Code¶

PyDy exists because of support provided by multiple Google Summers of Code. We still apply for student positions annual, both as an independent organization and through other orgs (e.g., SymPy, Python Software Foundation). We maintain a separate Wiki on GitHub for all GSoC related documents.
